About No Nic Nanny

Do you think your teen has been vaping? In recent years, vapes have become a staple in our teen's pockets, with tasty flavors, easy access, and affordable prices. However, not only is it flagrantly illegal for people their age, but they fail to realize that they are damaging their health, at the expense of "being cool".
In addition, we wanted the device to be comprehensive, also targeting smoking of any kind with a smoke detector.

Ultimately, us parents are worried. We don't want our kids to develop an addiction to this potential gateway. And the home is where addictions can develop.

So, we made NoNicNanny. Simply place the device in your teen's room, and it will notify you via text if vape or smoke is detected in the vicinity. It will also keep a record of every time the device was triggered.
